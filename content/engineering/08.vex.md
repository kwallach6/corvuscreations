# Vex Robotics

![vex-robot](/vex/vex-robot.jpg)

My first true engineering experience was VEX Robotics.  In 2021, Stargate High School established its VEX Robotics team, and I had the opportunity to be the primary builder/mechanical designer for Subteam A.  This team attended two competitions during the 2021-2022 "Tipping Point" season, and while no Stargate Teams won awards or advanced to later competition rounds that year, Stargate has sent at least one VEX team to the VEX World Championship every year since and has won numerous awards.  While I was only part of Stargate's first season, the lessons learned were incredibly relevant to everything I worked on at RPI.  Everything is documented in the team's [Engineering Design Notebook](/vex/32092A-Engineering-Notebook-2021-2022.pdf).
