# Rensselaer Concert Choir

![A photo of the choir performing](/choir/choir.png){:placeholder='[1067, 803]' height="803" preload="" sizes="100vw lg:1024px" width="1067"}

RPI's choir was founded in 1876, and I sang Tenor 1 for three years with this incredible ensemble!  Additionally, through the choir and its director, Dr. Robert Whalen, I had the opportunity to rehearse with Albany Pro Musica and the world-renowned Dr. Jose Daniel Flores-Caraballo, meet the famous Midori, and watch the Concordia College choir perform on tour in Albany.  Check out some of the Rensselaer Concert Choir's performances below, along with the Rensselaer Orchestra and the Rensselaer Wind Symphony.
