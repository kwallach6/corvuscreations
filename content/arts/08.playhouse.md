# The RPI Playhouse: Rendered

![A 3D rendering of the RPI Playhouse balcony](/playhouse/Balcony.png)

The final project for my 3D Digital Modeling course was to model and texture an environment or a character, and to give full detail to at least one feature.  For this project, I modeled the RPI Playhouse, mostly from memory and a small set of reference photos I collected from Instagram posts of previous shows.  This project took 3 months, and the final rendering shows the house empty, with the set from *The Lightning Thief* on stage, lit according to actual show paperwork.  Check out a gallery of renderings and reference photos from this project!

# The RPI Playhouse: Augment3d

After I learned Augment3d during the 2025 Evening of Performance, I imported an FBX of this 3D model into Augment3d, placed and focused all of the lights, and made the main curtain moveable to create an interactive venue for teaching lighting design.  I then recreated my lighting design from the 2023 Evening of Performance on this virtual set from memory, as everything from the Playhouse version of that show was destroyed in the flood.
