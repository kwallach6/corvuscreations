# 8th Wonder Spring Showcase

![mq2](/8th-wonder/mq2.jpg)

As part of the RPI Union Show Techs, I had the opportunity to lighting design a song in the 8th Wonder Spring Showcase, a K-Pop dance show.  I also volunteered to refocus the front wash and create Vectorworks documentation of the West Hall Auditorium.  This show was programmed in GrandMA3 on a 3D model of the stage and kept organized by using a separate sequence for each song and programming cues using recipes.  Each sequence was then timecoded to the respective sound file to mostly automate the show run process.

## Lighting Design Video

This video was taken during my final runthrough before the dancers were onstage.  At this point, all of my cues were complete and timecoded to the music.

## Documentation

The West Hall Auditorium documentation includes a 2D lighting plot, 3D venue model, instrument schedule, and channel hookup.  The front wash was refocused into 5 pairs of R02/R60 warm/cool washes, with each fixture having R119 frost to improve blend.  I also built a side lighting system with 2 booms, each with a shin, mid, and head level fixture.
