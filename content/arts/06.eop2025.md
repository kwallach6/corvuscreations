# Evening of Performance 2025

![IMG-9676](/eop-2025/IMG-9676.jpg)

The 2025 Evening of Performance with the RPI Players was quite literally the definition of theatre on a shoestring.  This production featured a number of RPI's performing arts clubs, as well as Shakespeare's *Comedy of Errors* presented by the RPI Players.  This play was chosen, as there was no budget for performance rights and almost no budget for tech.  I was the master electrician for this show, and this was my first time programming lights in Augment3d and creating documentation in Vectorworks.  My lighting designer, Mariana Gavilan, was also new to the department, so this really became a production of firsts!  Check out some of the documentation that made this show run so smoothly, as this was one of the only RPI Players shows where lighting was finished ahead of schedule and everything just worked on the first try!
